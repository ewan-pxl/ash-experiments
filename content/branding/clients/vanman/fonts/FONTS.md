## Rules

- `.woff2` for web, `.otf`/`.ttf` for design tooling.
- ⚠️ Check the licence before committing the file. If redistribution is not
  permitted, do **not** commit the font — instead record here where to obtain it
  and under what licence.
- Keep family names here consistent with `tokens/typography.tokens.json`.
